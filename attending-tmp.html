Hotels

Conference hotel (Rhiga Royal Hotel Kyoto)

We have tentatively reserved 40 rooms for 2 nights (December 1st and 2nd) and are doing final tuning for the reservation system. Perhaps we will be able to offer the room at a little less than 15,000 JPY/Night. It is expected that the reservation will start at around October 10th, 2019..

Other hotels

Since the period of our symposium is a very high season in Kyoto, if you cannot find an inexpensive hotel, we recommend hotels at Osaka or Shiga prefecture from which you can arrive at Kyoto station within half an hour.
